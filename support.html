Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Block Blast?

Block Blast is a color-matching puzzle game. To play, simply click on groups of 2 or more adjacent blocks of the same color. These blocks will disappear, and the blocks above will fall down to fill the empty spaces. Your goal is to reach the target score for each level before running out of moves.

How do I advance to the next level?

To advance to the next level, you need to reach the target score displayed at the top of the game screen. Once you reach this score, you'll automatically progress to the next level with a new, higher target score.

Why is the game getting harder?

Each level introduces progressively more challenging gameplay. The target scores increase, and at higher levels, new block colors are introduced (pink at level 3, teal at level 5). This makes the game more challenging and engaging as you progress!

Does Block Blast save my progress?

Yes, Block Blast saves your highest level reached using your browser's session storage. This means your progress will be saved as long as you don't clear your browser data or use private browsing mode. If you close your browser and return later, you'll be able to continue from your highest unlocked level.

The game isn't working properly on my device. What should I do?

If you're experiencing technical issues with Block Blast, try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Refresh your browser page
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.)
  • Ensure your browser is updated to the latest version
  • Check your internet connection
