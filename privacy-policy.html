Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 26, 2025

At Block Zone, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you play our free browser game "Block Blast" or visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you access our game, we may automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • Device type and browser information
  • IP address
  • Operating system
  • Time spent playing the game
  • Game progress and scores
  • Browser cookies and local storage data

1.2 Game Data

When you play Block Blast, we temporarily store gameplay data such as:

  • Game progress (current level and score)
  • Game statistics and performance metrics
  • Session duration

This data is stored locally on your device using browser session storage and is not permanently stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes, including:

  • Providing and maintaining our game service
  • Improving and optimizing game performance
  • Understanding usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Monitoring the technical functioning of our game
  • Detecting and preventing technical issues

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our game and website and to hold certain information to enhance your experience.

Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier. These are sent to your browser from a website and stored on your device. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the game to function properly
  • Preference Cookies: Allow the game to remember choices you make
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our game

4. Third-Party Services

Our game may include third-party code and libraries that collect information to provide additional functionality. These third parties have access to your information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Analytics services (like Google Analytics) to track and measure game usage
  • CDN services to deliver game assets efficiently
  • Font libraries to enhance visual design

5. Data Retention

Block Blast primarily uses browser session storage and local storage to maintain your game progress. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers for long-term storage. Your game progress and settings will be retained until you clear your browser cache or local storage.

6. Children's Privacy

Our game does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of your personal information
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to object to processing of your personal information
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
